Awards and Recognitions

The leading food producer "Azersun Holding" and its founder and leader Abdolbari Goozal have been awarded with awards and medals by hundreds of local and international organizations, the highest state organizations, and various rating agencies for achievements gained during the years of activity. Abdolbari Goozal who distinguishes himself by his humbleness, thinks of awarded prizes very interestingly. He always says: "The greatest reward for me is the faith, trust, and confidence that people put in us". It is noteworthy that Abdolbari Goozal who has made significant contributions to the establishment of modern Azerbaijani industry is the first business person awarded with “Progress” medal the by President of Azerbaijan.

"Azersun Holding", which sensitively approaches the research and innovation, is acknowledged by the community for achievements in the relevant sphere. The heads of various states who get familiarized with Holding’s experience which has become a desirable model by all, invite the head of the Group Abdolbari Goozal to their countries. Regardless of the geography of activity, "Azersun Holding" and its staff are capable of becoming favorites, compound part, friends, and colleagues of that space. And, it stems from the ethics and philosophy of "Azersun Holding" which has been architected by Abdolbari Goozal. The company is a carrier of universal values and selecting servicing to every human-being and the humanity as its objective, has always been highly appreciated by the community. The article which is submitted to your kind attention does not regard the prizes which "Azersun Holding" has won, but the philosophy behind this success. All products manufactured by the company, thanks to professional management, courage, and honesty, have won consumers’ sympathy, which is the biggest prize. It is very gratifying for all of us that "Azersun Holding"-owned national brands have gone beyond the national borders, the products labeled as "Made in Azerbaijan" bring fame to our country around the world. We can confidently say that "Azersun Holding", being home to about 10 thousand members, justifies the confidence it has obtained, and will gain more and more successes in the coming years and make significant contributions to the development of national food industry.
